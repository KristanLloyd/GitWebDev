About the Coffee and Café

Our coffee is unique because every part of our menu is with filipino touch.
We want our customers to have connection with our offerings,


Sitio Cafe started unexpectedly. We just thought of starting a pop up business and it came out well.
Now, we are thriving and going strong.
The name Sitio is a spanish word that means Location or Street.
We came up with the idea of naming it Sitio Cafe, a lowkey and to go place for coffee for all.
